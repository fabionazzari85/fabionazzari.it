What data is collected

  • Identification and contact data such as first name, last name, email and phone number.
  • Business or operational data such as company name, city, country, address, website and Instagram profile.
  • Request information such as type, volume of interest and free message.
  • Technical data linked to form submission, such as source page and request context.

Purposes of processing

  • To contact you again regarding the request you sent.
  • To manage leads, quotations, commercial contacts and operational follow-up.
  • To organise information inside the CRM and internal dashboard for faster handling.

Legal basis

Processing is based on the consent expressed through the checkbox in the form before submitting the request.

Processing methods

Data is processed using digital and organisational tools designed to limit access to authorised staff or collaborators involved in handling the request.

Data retention

Data is stored for the time required to manage the request and any resulting relationship, unless different legal obligations apply.

Data subject rights

You may request access, rectification, update, restriction or deletion of your data by writing to info@nazzari-iseo.it.
